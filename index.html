Our story

A family business across two oceans

J3S is run by the Lising family — Jeffrey, an automation engineer, and Sushiela, a registered nurse, based in the US with their two children: Shaun, also a registered nurse, and Sheyenne, an artist. They built J3S in the Philippines remotely, with one conviction:

“If no one sacrifices to build a business, who will create employment?”

Why water came first. Their families live here — and they saw firsthand that not all “purified” water is equal. Containers left out in the sun. Machines too weak to truly clean the source water. No regular lab testing. Water is vital to health, so J3S was built to do it properly.

That same standard — do it right, take care of people — now runs through our water, ice, and laundry branches, and every Filipino job they create at home.
