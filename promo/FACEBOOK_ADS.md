           Text overlay: "Yelo."

           Text overlay: "Labada."

           Text overlay: "Linis. Lamig. Bango. — J3S"
5. (14–20s) End card: use `j3s-reel-story-1080x1920.png`
           Voice/text: "8 branches. Open 7AM–7PM. Order online — scan o
           i-click ang link sa comments!"

Post the website URL as the FIRST comment and pin it.
Suggested audio: any trending upbeat Filipino track (pick from the
Reels audio library so it doesn't get muted).

## Posting tips

- Post the square image + Caption 1 on the "Jthrees Tubeice" page, then
  share it to local community groups (Biñan/Carmona/Southville buy & sell).
- Pin the launch post to the top of the page.
- Boost later if desired: even ₱100–200 on a 10km radius around each
  branch reaches thousands of nearby households.
- When the j3s.ph domain arrives, regenerate the QR images (ask Claude)
  and update captions.
